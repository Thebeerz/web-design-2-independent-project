About class Bee

Class Bee is an annual event that invites all for a weekend of inspiration and creativity! We gather the best creatives in town to teach you their expertise in their various workshops.

Founded by a group of art friends, our mission is to teach all things visual arts: painting, tattooing, printmaking, you name it!
